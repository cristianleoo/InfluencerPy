# Examples

This section contains practical tutorials and real-world examples to help you get the most out of InfluencerPy.

## Available Examples

### [Daily AI Paper Feed](daily-ai-papers.md) 📄
Learn how to set up an RSS Scout that monitors the latest AI research papers from arXiv and automatically drafts engaging social media posts about cutting-edge research.

**What you'll learn:**
- Finding and subscribing to academic RSS feeds
- Configuring an RSS Scout for research content
- Scheduling daily automated posts
- Customizing the AI's analysis and writing style
